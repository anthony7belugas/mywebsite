ü¶Ü Bird Flu Tracker

Interactive H5N1 Avian Influenza Data Visualization

Overview

Since 2003, the CDC has reported over 950 H5N1 cases globally with a 49% mortality rate. This visualization helps researchers and policymakers identify when, where, and why outbreaks occur, providing critical insights for public health decision-making.

Key Insights

  • Winter drives surges ‚Äî Inverse relationship between temperature and case counts, with cold months seeing highest outbreak rates
  • Wetland species at highest risk ‚Äî Mallards and Canada Geese face greatest danger due to migration patterns and habitat
  • Regional hotspots ‚Äî Minnesota and California show elevated cases based on ecological factors and migration routes

Technology Stack

React, Vite, React Router, Interactive Mapping Libraries, SQL Database Backend, Data Visualization

ü¶ã HeatShield NanoWing Pro

Biomimicry-Inspired Athletic Cooling Technology

The Problem

Athletes face a hidden performance barrier: just 1¬∞C change in muscle temperature can reduce performance by up to 5%. Traditional shin guards trap heat during gameplay, causing discomfort, reduced performance, and potential injury risk.

Our Solution

Inspired by butterfly wing nanostructures that naturally release heat to prevent overheating during flight, we developed the NanoWing Pro ‚Äî a 3D-printed TPU shin guard with biomimetic cooling properties that significantly outperforms traditional EVA foam designs.

Validation & Results

  • User Research: Interviewed 7 soccer players ‚Äî 5/7 reported significant heat/sweat issues
  • Market Validation: 100% of respondents willing to pay 15-25% premium for cooling tech
  • Testing: Heat testing confirmed faster cooling than leading competitors
  • Pitch: Presented to Ravi Sawhney at RKS Design during Innovators Forum

Team
